About me

I am a Ph.D. student at UCLA advised by Professor Guido Montúfar, where I work on statistical machine learning problems with a focus on foundations of deep learning.

Prior to graduate school, I did bachelors in Mathematics at SUNY Stony Brook and The University of Hong Kong. I was fortunate to be advised by Professor Robert Lazarsfeld who introduced me to the wonderful world of classical algebraic geometry.

Here are topics I am currently working on and/or hope to explore in the recent future:

  • Deep learning theory: Generalization, Non-convex optimization

  • Algebraic statistics

  • Manifold learning
